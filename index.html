Nice to meet you!

I am a Computing student with a special background.

I do not only like computers. I am also passionate about writing, creative digital media, marketing and intercultural connections.

What is special about me?


Creativity

I have profound experience with journalistic writing, editing as well as photography and audio-visual media technology. I love establishing strategies for social media marketing producing professional content for Facebook and Instagram. Simply put, I love to create!

Intercultural Competence

For me, there is nothing more interesting than getting to know other cultures! I would love to work in an intercultural context, in which I can use the experiences I have made in two semesters abroad and in mentoring foreign students in Germany.

Leadership

For one year I was allowed to lead a team of twenty people as the director of the editorial departement of a student association called Feki.de. I loved discussing and making decisions which had impact on the over 100 members of the society.
